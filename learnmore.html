PROJECT OVERVIEW

The Problem

Most users have a hectic schedule outside work/school. They have items that can be donated but lack the time and energy to take them to the donation center. The living space becomes cluttered with unused items.

The Solution

An application like “GIVE AWAY FOR GOOD” will help them pick up the items for donation and offers the option for resale some of their items through our app as well.

My Role

I collaborated with other UX/UI designers on the empathy, definition, and ideation phases of the project, then I planned, wireframed, and prototyped the UI.

Tools

Figma, Miro, Adobe Photoshop

EMPATHIZE

USER RESEARCH

We aim to create a successful mobile application to help and solve the issues for most users who have busy schedules but want to do good deeds by giving their gently used items to those in need and the potential of earning a bit of money back if they wish to resale some of those items. 

PROTO PERSONA

SURVEY DATA

SURVEY DATA AND INTERVIEWS

We have conducted 5 interviews and got 10 responses from surveys. The majority have busy schedules outside work/school. More than 50% take their children’s gently used clothes to the donation center. More than 70% have at least 1-2 boxes of the items for donation. Many of them would go to the donation center every few years or so. Most participants do not have large items or furniture for donation and they mostly move twice in the past ten year.

DEFINE

USER INSIGHT
& PROBLEM STATEMENT

Throughout interviews and surveys users were motivated by helping others and mentioned time constraints as a major pain point, especially when moving. Therefore, we believe that users could benefit from an app that would take care of the donation for them, and offer users the possibility to make a profit. How might we help users donate/resale their items with minimal time/energy?. 

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Most users have a hectic schedule outside work/school. They have items that can be donated, but lack time and energy to take them to the donation center. Having an application like “GIVE AWAY FOR GOOD” to help them pick up the item would be ideal for solving the issues. On top of that the users will have the option for resale some of their items through our app as well.
